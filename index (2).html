About Me

Passionate about product. Empathetic to people.

Hi, I am Aziza! I am a student who genuinely loves the human side of building products. I am passionate about product, deeply empathetic to users, and a very good listener. I believe the best ideas come from truly hearing what people need, not just what they say.

I bring a mix of joy and professionalism to everything I do. Whether running user interviews, crafting a campaign, or brainstorming a roadmap, I show up with curiosity, warmth, and the genuine belief that great products start with great empathy.

I study at Minerva University ↗, one of the most unique universities in the world, where students move to a new city every year, living and learning across San Francisco, Seoul, Berlin, Buenos Aires, London, and beyond. It has shaped how I think about people, culture, and building for global audiences.

I recently built PeerLink ↗ because I watched my own peers struggle to connect with each other. They were experiencing FOMO, anxiety, and real barriers around reaching out. PeerLink is being built to help students remove those fears and connect meaningfully.
